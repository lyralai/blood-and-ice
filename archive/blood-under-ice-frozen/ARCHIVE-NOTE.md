# Blood Under Ice (frozen draft) — ARCHIVED

This is the superseded **frozen-island** draft (formerly the canonical Book One,
"Blood Under Ice"). It has been replaced as Book 1 by the tropical manuscript at
`manuscript/book-one-wild-boy/`, and the series has been retitled **Ice and Blood**.

**Why it's kept (do not delete):** this draft is a deep **design quarry for Books
2–3**. Its real value is its political/world bible and later-arc structure, not its
early prose:

- the four-tribe political machine (Khar Vane / Salt Veyr / Fen-Kin / Hollow Antler)
  → Book 2's **blood-city factions**
- the **White Pit** → Book 2's tropical **blood-arena**
- the Guest / blood-force **corruption stages, costs, and limits** → the Red (climate-neutral; carry forward)
- the **Rauth lineage / 30-year isolation** history → the **hunted bloodline**
- the systems/ledger "rise through the machine" arc (Parts 5–6) → Book 2's **blood-economy** rise

When designing Books 2–3, mine this draft and its docs (`docs/world/four-tribes.md`,
`docs/world/blood-and-ice-magic.md`, the continuity dossiers) for political and
structural material, **converted to the tropical/blood canon**.

Nothing here is current canon. See `/README.md`, `/BOOK-ONE.md`, and
`docs/story/series-outline-3book.md` for the live project.
