## Voice Fix Bible Addendum (For Future Drafters)

**Purpose:** 1-2 page concrete reference. Use before/after to train the feral anchor. Apply to all future closing reflections and The Red processing (especially post-crown systems phases).

**Core Principle (from voice-guide + protagonist.md):**  
Rook notices with his body first. Politics arrives as what the eyes see, what the hands want to do, what the iron tastes like, who will not meet the gaze, what the weather does to the wound, which exit is still open. Reflection is allowed only as immediate physical reaction + what he does next. Guest is pressure, heat, laughter, intrusive images, almost-words, instinctive certainty. The more articulate it becomes, the more worried the reader should be. Success (restraint, caller, named claims) teaches it — show the teaching in the body's cost (sweat, bite, red edges, spear grip, self-name repetition), not in clean summary.
