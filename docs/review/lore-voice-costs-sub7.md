The dual inheritance creates a tightening spiral: every restraint teaches the Guest new shapes (law, counting, caller, small loop); every activation widens the gap between boy and myth. Cold side provides the only reliable brake; berserk side grows more articulate and patient under pressure.

   Ice-Blood: raw cold resistance, animal attention.  
   Wound-Guest: pressure under ribs, sharpened senses on injury.  
   Voice: short, suspicious, exit-focused. No self-name yet.

2. **Terror (ch9-10, immediate post-Pit)**  
   Ice-Blood: memory gap + first ancestral flash (red hands on ice).  
   Wound-Guest: full takeover, laughter, violent healing, public myth ownership loss.  
   Voice: fractured — sensory clarity mixed with "I do not remember climbing out."

3. **Experiment (ch12-15)**  
   Ice-Blood: deliberate yielding, name as brake.  
   Wound-Guest: kill impulses offered as "clean lines"; learns restraint shape.  
   Voice: longer sentences when dread accumulates; self-name appears as tool.

4. **Instrument (ch17-19)**  
   Ice-Blood: holds name through second pit night.  
   Wound-Guest: strategic half-red (Blood Snow), almost-words.  
   Voice: public meaning begins to layer over private suspicion.

5. **Contamination (ch20-25)**  
   Ice-Blood: bone band as visible term + memory intrusion on broth heat.  
   Wound-Guest: learns "Store", counting language, "Names have bellies".  
   Voice: bored by clean politics; only violence/hunger engages the Guest.

6. **Negotiation (ch26-32)**  
   Ice-Blood: wrapped-spear discipline, self-name + cheek-bite + witness counting as anchors.  
   Wound-Guest: "Please" plea (ch27), strategic patience, debt-bowl filing, small-loop name learning, caller-waiting.  
   Voice: internal siege visible — short commands to self ("Rook"), iron taste, red at vision edges, hand shaking on spear after recession.

7. **Crisis (ch33+)**  
   Ice-Blood: systems (cradle tenth, road-sworn, small loop, winter-sworn) become the only brake.  
   Wound-Guest: turns law into slower/larger meal (tribute before ice, due line, proposers carry count).  
   Voice: night-walk reflections on "small loop heavier than the cord"; island larger than camps; more voices = thinner center. The boy still owns the name, but the cost of ownership is now administrative hunger the Guest can wait inside.

## Voice Examples by Stage (Post-Unlock Focus)

### Stage 2-3 (Immediate Post-Pit Terror → Experiment)
> The red heat laughed under my ribs while I climbed. I did not remember the climb. Only the taste of old blood and the sound of something that was not me laughing through my mouth. When I reached the rim the four tribes were already kneeling. I wanted to tell them to stop. The thing wanted them to stay down forever.

### Stage 4-5 (Instrument → Contamination)
> They called it the blood-crown. The bone band sat on my head like a second set of teeth. The Wound-Guest pressed against it from inside, testing the fit. "Not food," it said when the storehouse opened. "Store." I bit my tongue until iron cut the wanting and answered with my own name. The red thread on the wrapped spear bit my palm in answer. Success teaches it, Asha said. She was not wrong.

### Stage 6 (Negotiation — Current Frontier)
> The small loop felt heavier than the cord that held it. Reed-Bend girl pressed her basket hard against her hip when the Guest rose on "cough" and "weak." I said my name once, under my breath, and counted the witnesses aloud so the thing would hear them too. Maren. Harvek. Asha. Mother Harl. Keva. The girl with the basket. The Guest settled with a low internal laugh. It had noted the shape of the due line for later. My hand would not stop shaking on the spear shaft long after the pressure passed.

> "The hand that had wanted to close was the one that now carried the count." I walked the spur that night with the wrapped spear across my back. The island was larger than the camps. Every new voice under the name was another place for other hands to reach before the knot could hold. The Wound-Guest waited inside the law I had just made. It was patient. It had learned that names could pull stores without the red-state rising if the boy made the ones who wanted meat carry the collection themselves.
