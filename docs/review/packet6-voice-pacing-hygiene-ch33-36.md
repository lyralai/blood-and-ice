**Packet 6 Voice Rules (One-Pager Extension of Voice Fix Bible)**

**Core Principle (from voice-guide + protagonist.md + ch21-36 gate):**  
Rook notices with his body first. Politics arrives as what the eyes see, what the hands want to do, what the iron tastes like, who will not meet the gaze, what the weather does to the wound, which exit is still open. Reflection is allowed only as immediate physical reaction + what he does next. Guest is pressure, heat, laughter, intrusive images, almost-words, instinctive certainty. The more articulate it becomes, the more worried the reader should be. In Packet 6 (Winter Ledger: small loops, due, reckonings, living debt stretched, caller under load), success (restraint, named claims, turned factions) teaches the Guest — show the teaching in the body's cost and the concrete images the Guest offers, never in clean summary sentences in the close.
