## Flagged Examples — Worst 4-6 + Tightened Versions

**1. ch29 Open Store (worst multi-sentence strategic)**  
Original (too articulate):  
"The inlet, it offered. Boats have oil. Boats have names that eat. ... Names have bellies, it said. The counters lie with numbers. Open the inlet with teeth and the cradles will not need to ask again. The littlest will feed the story if we take what the boats hide. Their small mouths will make the crown strong."

**Tightened version (image + corrupted concept + pressure):**  
The inlet pressed at him, warm and certain. Oil skins under Khar hands. Boats that bent. The littlest mouths would carry the story farther than any board if the hand closed on what the counters hid. Names have weight. The red heat climbed. He tasted iron and counted witnesses until the wanting pulled back one step.

**2. ch31 / ch34 Useful Sacrifice / Dremm's Offer (repeated "This is not waste..." sentence)**  
Original (ch31):  
*This is not waste. Fear is the store that does not freeze. Tribute before the ice takes the name.*

Original (ch34 variant + meta):  
*This is not waste. Fear is the store that does not freeze. Tribute before the ice takes the name.* ... The hand that had wanted to close was the one that now carried the count.

**Tightened (image + single tainted concept):**  
The images came patient and clean: the debt bowl carried to the storehouse door and poured so every future tally would carry the mark of what had been paid in flesh. The three standing thin but alive. Fear that would bring baskets before the snow locked the spur. It was not waste. It was the store that did not freeze. The heat turned the pictures like a man testing a new knife and filed the shape of the bowl traveling.

**3. ch35 Wrapped Call (full bargaining sentence + meta door insight)**  
Original:  
Let the spear-mother name the bend as yours in blood. I will close the hands on the ones who reach before the knot. Three turnings. The small will feed the name. ... the wrapped call is not only a leash but a door.

**Tightened:**  
The caller rhythm it had learned in the fog woods. The spear-mother could name the bend in blood and the hand would close on the ones who reached first. The small would feed the name. Three turnings. The red pressed until the vision was only edges. It wanted the rhythm. It wanted the door the wrapped thing had shown it. He bit until iron came and spoke the name until the tide pulled back.

**4. ch33 Cradle Reckoning (clean administrative governance)**  
Original:  
Reed-Bend first, it offered. Small is still small. Cut the loop and the others will see the crown knows which mouths matter. ... The name can starve its own promises and still grow stronger. The ones who watch will learn the crown knows which mouths close first under frost.

**Tightened:**  
Reed-Bend first. Small is still small. Cut the loop and the others would see. The cradles that fed the story could be left thin; the guards who carried teeth would remember who fed them when the snow locked the road. The red offered the shape of the choice traveling farther than meat. He gripped the spear until the thread bit and spoke the small loop aloud until the wanting settled.

**5. ch30 Naim (overly poetic vision)**  
Original:  
wind moving through black trees, carrying names that were not mine into every cradle frame on the ridge, names that made the wood whistle like the dead were still trying to speak. Then it showed me my own mouth forced open, something that was not my voice naming the island's hungers one by one while the cradles drank the sound.

**Tightened:**  
The wind through the black trees. Names not his moving into every cradle frame until the wood whistled like the dead still spoke. His own mouth forced open, the island's hungers named by a voice that was not his while the frames drank. The red climbed like a closing hand. He bit his cheek until iron held and counted the ridge path, the store bar, the knife he would not draw.

**6. ch27 "Please" plea (too relational)**  
Original: The Red begged. Please, it almost said. That was the worst thing it had done yet. Not command. Not hunger. Plea.

**Tightened:**  
The Red surged with the old male challenge and offered the clean shape of it: Harvek's throat under the hand that was not a boy's. It pressed forward, warm and wanting, the public ground perfect. Not command. Wanting. He said his name until the heat slowed and the wanting pulled back one step, leaving the shape of the plea behind like a taste.
