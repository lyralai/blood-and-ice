## Safe Escalation Ladder (Book One, with Crisis Notes)

**Level 0-1 (ch1-20)**: Unchanged from v2 (reflex → early images + single alien certainties). Physical cost mandatory.

**Level 2 — Post-Crown Learning Phase (ch21-36 ceiling, per v2)**: 
- **Allowed**: Clear non-verbal images; single almost-words/tasted concepts ("Store", "Door", "small loop", "road's due", names tasted); strategic hunger via images + pressure + low internal reactions ("noted the shape", low laugh-like feeling, patient settling).
- **Strictly Limited**: Short broken almost-phrases only under extreme pressure + visible anchor failure (e.g. "Please" as plea; "Kill caller" as shape). Image-distorted reframes ("Names have bellies").
- **Forbidden**: Full grammatical sentences; clean strategic plans ("This is not waste. Fear is the store that does not freeze. Tribute before the ice takes the name."); bargaining/meta tool insight ("Let the spear-mother name... I will close the hands..."; "the wrapped call is not only a leash but a door"; pushing repetition back at caller); relational negotiation; clean administrative solutions offered as governance.

**Level 2.5 — Crisis Phase Ceiling (ch37-38 public rite, this v3)**: 
The live test under winter failure (inlet frozen, child death from prior taking, faction reach, small loop cord cut, "road's due" claim made public, four-tribe + small + road-sworn + cradle witnesses present, crown term spoken aloud again). Guest has learned from ch21-36 systems (store vs food, debt bowl as tool, small loop as claim the center must answer, "the road's due" as law price, caller rhythm as anticipatable door). This learning **positions it to exploit** the public rite, but the ceiling holds: exploitation must remain corrupted by appetite and image, not clean speech.

**Allowed in ch37 crisis (weaponize prior learning without breaking ceiling)**:
- Surge on named systems from prior scenes ("the road's due", "small loop", the wrapped call rhythm, the debt bowl traveling, cradles that "feed the story").
- Intrusive images that are appetite-corrupted and strategic but **not grammatical**: e.g. the flats closed in a fist until the due comes in fear; the small sending the rest of the eel/reed because the hand that counted them now owes; the debt bowl carried to the bend fires so every watcher sees the mark the crown named; coughing children as the ones who will make the name strong if the boy slips; the caller's rhythm as a shape the red can answer before the third strike lands.
- Single tainted almost-concepts or tasted names repeated with pressure ("the due the boy named", "the small that the count must feed", "the door the wrapped thing showed").
- Low internal reactions after Rook anchors: patient settling, low laugh-like feeling that the shape was noted, warmth that the call has a rhythm it can wait inside.
- Physical override that shows learning (knees bending toward the spring before the third strike; hand reaching for the spear in Asha's hands; voice starting to answer the name before Rook bites it back).
- Boredom/disinterest in clean counting or wound care remains protective — it forces Rook to handle the "due" claim and the witnesses alone until the red rises.

**Strictly Limited / Borderline in ch37 (use only with extreme visible cost + anchor failure)**:
- One short, broken almost-phrase under maximum pressure (e.g. "Close it" or "The due" as raw shape/certainty, never a full clause). Must be immediately countered by Rook's physical anchoring + public naming of the law term.
- Image that arrives almost as a plan but is visibly corrupted (e.g. the image of the bend girl with the basket pressed to her hip until the eel dries hard, then the red offering the shape of the due paid in the one who reached first).

**Strictly Forbidden in ch37 (and until deliberate later pivot)**:
- Any full grammatical sentence or clean strategic statement from the Guest's "voice" ("This is not waste"; "Fear is the store that does not freeze"; "Tribute before the ice takes the name"; "The name can starve its own promises and still grow stronger").
- Bargaining language or explicit offers ("Let the spear-mother name the bend as yours in blood. I will close the hands... Three turnings. The small will feed the name.").
- Meta-understanding of tools ("the wrapped call is not only a leash but a door"; "the call has a shape and the crown has a door"; pushing the name-repetition back at Asha/Cairn as "Rook calls the spear-mother").
- Clean administrative/governance solutions ("Cut the loop and the others will see"; "Prioritize the ones who feed the name in teeth"; "The hand that wanted to close is the one that now carries the count").
- Relational pleas or second-personality negotiation.
- The Guest "speaking" a full plan that Rook then resists in dialogue form. Resistance must happen inside the image pressure + bodily cost.

**How prior learning (admin language, small loop, "the due", caller anticipation) is allowed to position exploitation in ch37 without over-articulation**:
- The Guest surges hardest on the public naming of "the road's due" (from ch36) and the wrapped call rhythm (from ch26/35 training). Post-recession: hand shaking visibly on spear or stone, blood on stone/snow, longer red clearance, public dread from young Khar who wanted the old price.

## Mandatory Cost & Visibility Rules (Crisis Enforcement — Heavier than v2)

1. **Physical toll always shown and escalated for ch37**: Iron taste flooding, sweat at hairline even in ice, red at edges like rising water or second eyes, voice strain/cracking publicly, hand shaking on spear after recession (worse than ch35), biting cheek/tongue/lip until blood runs visibly, knees bending toward spring before the third strike, pressure climbing ribs like closing ice or a hand testing the due knot. After-effect lingers into the night walk or next public moment (tremor when holding the re-tied cord, red at vision edges when looking at the bend girl).
2. **Active anchoring required and visible to all**: Self-name ("Rook who holds the small loop", "Rook who named the due", "Rook who came back") spoken internally and at least once aloud where witnesses can hear or see the effort. Counting witnesses/exits/the due claim aloud. Wrapped spear grip (red thread bite drawing blood). Bone band. Public naming of the law term being defended ("The road's due is the law's price. Not the pit's meat." — Rook says this, not the Guest).
3. **Witness & political cost mandatory and public**: Every near-use or surge in the rite must visibly cost follower loyalty (young Khar / Dremm knot / Half-Ear faction waiting for the boy to slip so the old price can be asked again), widen the gap between boy and myth (Asha stepping back, Cairn's hand on bone blade, Naim's second silence on the bend dead), or teach external checks (Maren's thin mouth on the stretched boards; Mother Harl naming the order back; the bend girl pressing the basket harder; Harvek's lesson landing on his own knot again). The crown term (Fen may call or bind/kill) must be spoken aloud again by a witness (Solek or Asha) under the pressure.
4. **Learning is shown, not told — and the education is double-edged**: Guest "files" or "notes the shape" of the due, the call rhythm, the small loop as obligation via images + low internal pleasure/laugh + later behavior (e.g. the hand that wanted to close now has to carry the due in living debt; the call that was a leash now has a rhythm it can wait inside). Never via clean exposition or the Guest offering a plan. The reader sees the cost through Rook's isolation and the witnesses' reactions.
5. **Boredom with clean systems remains protective until the red rises**: The Guest disengages from pure counting or wound care; it only wakes on the named claim ("the road's due", the small that the count must feed, the call rhythm). This forces Rook to handle the public rite and the "due" demand alone until the pressure peaks.

## Trigger & Restraint Guidelines for ch37 Crisis

- Rises hardest on: public naming of "the road's due" or small loop claim under winter failure; the wrapped call rhythm being struck while the red is already at the edges (anticipation from ch35 training); faction pressure (Half-Ear/Keff offering the old price again); named small/weak (coughing children, the bend girl with the basket); restraint that denies immediate meat while the due claim is public.
- Restraint and training (wrapped spear, self-name, Fen caller) are the primary educators — the ch37 scene is the payoff where the Guest uses what the boy and Asha taught it. Every successful hold in prior scenes (ch26, ch35) has taught it a new shape it can wait inside or turn.
- Do not trigger on convenience. The surge must be earned by the material failure (child death, cord cut, inlet frozen, faction reach) and the public demand for the due.
- Post-use recession must leave visible, public after-effects: shaking hand on spear or the re-tied cord, blood on stone/snow, longer red clearance, Asha/Cairn/Maren/Harvek noting the distance or readiness, young Khar watching with a new kind of waiting.

## "Almost Articulate" Guardrails Specific to ch37 Public Rite

- Prefer: Image + bodily pressure + one tainted concept or tasted name from prior learning ("the due the boy named", "the small that the count must feed", "the rhythm the wrapped thing showed").
- Acceptable rare escalation: One broken almost-phrase only when anchor is visibly failing and physical cost is extreme (e.g. during the third strike while knees bend). Immediately countered by Rook speaking the law term aloud and counting.
- Never: Let the Guest speak a full strategic sentence, bargain, or meta insight that Rook then "resists." The resistance happens inside the image pressure + Rook's physical anchoring + the political cost visible to witnesses.
- When the Guest "offers" a solution via image, it must arrive corrupted by hunger (the due paid in the one who reached first; the small sending tribute because fear makes baskets generous; the call answered before the boy finishes the name) and be immediately countered by Rook's anchoring + public naming of the existing law term.
- The learning ceiling is the point: the better Rook builds the due claim, the small loop, the living debt, and the caller discipline to contain the thing, the more sophisticated a monster he is teaching to wait inside those same tools.
