## Core Components (from ch26)
- **Spear:** Ash-gray shaft, old, carved with shallow lines that circle but never touch. Bone blade (white as milk under ice). Gray cloth wrap from point to heel (no blade/wood visible when carried; "dead thing prepared for carrying").
- **Red Thread:** Tied below the bone blade. "Seemed ordinary until my blood noticed it. Then the world leaned." Cairn: "Do not look at the thread when she calls." Affects blood/Guest directly (brightens or draws notice; post-call "darker at the knot, as if the blood from my mouth had found it across the space").
- **Strike Rhythm:** Asha strikes butt against stone (black stone in ch26; counting stone in ch35). Once. Twice. Third. Sound "went into my teeth." Progressive name calls timed to strikes.
- **Name Repetition (Caller Discipline):** Calls use escalating full names tied to identity/lineage/survival: "Rook", "Rook of no shore", "Rook who came back" (ch26). In crisis: "Rook", "Rook of no shore", "Rook who holds what the red would close" (ch35, incorporating small-loop political weight).
- **Caller Role:** Asha (Fen-Kin old-blood trainer/check). Cairn as backup "knife" (tendon cut readiness if red drowns vessel; "Strong hand. Clean cut." noted by Guest). Public witnesses mandatory (Solek/Khar, Maren/Salt, Mother Harl/Hollow, small-loop parties, road-sworn, Yara). No act private.

## Activation & Call Sequence (ch26 training → ch35 public test)
1. Spear produced/unwrapped in presence of red pressure (already at edges from faction/small/weak/cough triggers).
2. Asha strikes + names. Guest rises immediately/hard (anticipates rhythm after ch26 training; "did not wait for the second strike").

5. Recession (or hold). Spear re-wrapped quickly.

## What the "Call" Does / Guest Response (Limits)
- **Rook-side:** Forces name-holding under pain/pressure. Ice-blood (Rauth grip from protagonist.md + magic.md) provides endurance to hold name when red narrows vision/pressure climbs ribs like closing hand/ice. Physical override: knees bend toward spring, iron taste flooding, sweat (even cold), red at edges of vision (like second eyes or rising water/tide), voice strain/cracking, hand on spear.
- **Guest-side:** Rises hard on call (likes names as "hooks to it... handles on meat"). Learns/anticipates rhythm from prior training ("the caller rhythm it had learned in the fog woods"). Offers corrupted strategic images/bargains that weaponize the call itself (ch35: "Let the spear-mother name the bend as yours in blood. I will close the hands on the ones who reach before the knot. Three turnings. The small will feed the name."). Pushes name-repetition back at caller ("Rook calls the spear-mother. The old blood answers the pit."). Tastes names, files "Door" shape. Settles with low internal "laugh-like feeling" / pleased warmth / "noted the shape for later use" when held.
- **Strict Limits (guest-behavior-rules-v2.md Book One ceiling + audit; no full sentences/meta in Guest voice for ch37):** Communicates via appetite, images (corrupted by hunger: coughing children as "easy meat to lift", tide flats "closed in fist until nothing moved", debt bowl poured so "every future tally carried the mark", small will "feed the name"), pressure, sudden knowing, one broken almost-word under extreme duress only. "Noted the shape", low laugh, patient settling. **Cannot:** full grammatical strategic sentences, clean plans, relational bargaining, meta tool-insight ("the wrapped call is not only a leash but a door" — flagged/ tightened in audit), solve logistics, create healthy loyalty, tenderness, or act without Rook filtering. Bored by clean admin (ch28 precedent). Every use/restraint educates it (success teaches "where the door is" — Asha ch26).
- **What Guest Can Do With the "Call" (weaponization per queue):** Anticipate and wait inside rhythm/door. Turn discipline (push repetition back; offer bargain using call to name small in blood for closing hand). Weaponize accumulated tools (due, call, living debt, small loop — e.g., small loop as claim Guest likes; debt bowl as mark on tallies; road's due as "name the due and the small send"; living debt as "useful" collection that feeds fear without immediate meat). Use crown answers/limits as shape. File for later (when boy slips under more voices = thinner center).

- **Self-Name (primary, repeated from ch27 road plea onward):** "Rook. Rook who came back. Rook of no shore. Rook who holds the small loop. Rook who holds what the red would close." Spoken internally/aloud on strikes or pressure peaks. Ties to ice-blood "ability to hold a name under pain."

- **Bodily (bite for own blood):** Bite tongue/cheek/lip until iron floods (own blood, not Guest's). Physical cost visible.
