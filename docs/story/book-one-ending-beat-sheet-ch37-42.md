## Refined Ending Arc Summary

The final movement delivers the mid-Book One peak crisis (live public test of the ch20 crown term — "Fen may call him back. If he does not return, bind or kill under the terms that made the crown" — under the exact pressure the boy-king problem has created). Systems (small loop, road-sworn living debt, counting, due, wrapped-spear caller) are asked to do more than they were built for. The Wound-Guest, educated by ch26 training + ch29-36 store language, small-loop weight, "the road's due", winter-sworn, and public naming, weaponizes the tools the boy used to contain it: it anticipates the caller rhythm, turns discipline back, offers strategic "clean" solutions using crown shapes (tribute via the due, cut weak claims first, answer the call in its own time). 

Rook holds via accumulated boy discipline + political cost awareness (self-name, wrapped spear red thread bite, counting witnesses aloud, naming the small loop/due publicly). The return carries visible new costs (deeper physical toll, Asha/Cairn distance, Naim fracture paid publicly or via small-loop cost owned). One visible fracture is paid in front of the same witnesses who saw the call test. Immediately after, the first undeniable external signal arrives (lone runner, washed-up token/dead with ice-blood marks, or small boat naming "the pit that answers with laughter" / Rauth line / "the blood that woke on the black isles"). Witnessed by the full group. The tribes must interpret the sign under the fresh cost of the call and ledger failure. Rook's closing feral reflection synthesizes the arc without polish: pit survival → crown under law → winter ledger strain → live call test where the Guest used the boy's own tools → first external eyes. The island is awake; the center is thinner because every new mouth the name must feed creates a place for other hands to reach before the knot holds. The following is earned but visibly held by living debt systems + Fen old-blood check + Salt logistics more than pit myth. The Guest has learned that law, the call, the due, and the small loop are doors it can stand inside. The boy-king problem has grown with every knot tied. The White Pit was only the beginning.

This closes Book One on the original promise at reasonable scale while seeding the long-series roadmap (Book Two: island consolidation under hunger crown pressure; Book Three: cold mainland).
