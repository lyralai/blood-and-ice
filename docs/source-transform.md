# Source Transform

## Why This Exists

The original repository contained a raw autonomous loop dump built around direct fanfiction material and later recursive output. This pass converts the useful creative core into an original project package.

## What Was Preserved

The following story atoms survived the transformation:

- a feral northern boy washed into an island survival story
- old blood awakening under extreme injury
- a monster-state that is blessing, curse, and political symbol
- a hard tribal society where fear can become legitimacy
- a turning point in a sacrificial pit
- the idea that leadership begins before the protagonist is morally ready
- the tension between animal survival, strategic rule, and ancient possession

## What Was Removed

The review surface removes:

- direct names, houses, places, titles, creatures, and lore from the prior fanfiction frame
- direct references to the prior media franchise
- direct dependence on the old royal-return arc
- repetitive late-loop material
- meta-recursive autonomous-loop artifacts
- style instructions that asked for imitation of named living authors

The old material remains recoverable through git history, but the branch surface is intentionally clean.

## What Was Rebuilt

The setting is now **The Blackjaw Isles**, a cold island chain with its own politics, religions, food pressures, and bloodline conflicts.

The protagonist is now **Rook**, not an inherited franchise character. His value comes from:

- ambiguous old northern ancestry
- wilderness adaptation
- willingness to observe before speaking
- unwanted bond with the Wound-Guest
- symbolic capture by Khar Vane after the White Pit

The monster is now **the Wound-Guest**, an ancient blood-presence. It is not just rage. It has memory, hunger, pattern recognition, and alien patience.

The first book now centers on island politics and religious capture, not a rush to a mainland throne.

## Editorial Stance

The repo should be judged as a foundation PR. It is doing the hard structural work first:

- make it original
- make the world coherent
- make the power costly
- make book one writable
- make the next manuscript pass obvious

The manuscript samples are proof of voice and direction, not the final target length.
